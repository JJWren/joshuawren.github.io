Information About Me

I currently work as a "full-stack" C#/.NET Developer. Previously, I worked in the aviation industry for a little over a decade starting in the military. It was in the military where I found my passion for technology and started building computers for me, my friends, and my family. The hunger for tech did not stop there! I continued by learning about cybersecurity through the University of Arizona's CAE-CO degree progressram (NSA-rated, Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Operations). I was exposed to and utilized a variety of regularly used industry tools from both an offensive and defensive perspective such as Wireshark, Metasploit, data forensics tools, x86 Assembly, Python, etc.


During my university coursework, I was laid off due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Whilst trying to maintain support my wife and four kids, I plunged into a coding bootcamp to aid my university studies and make a quicker transition to the tech fields. It was here where I was exposed to the software tools behind creating the websites we love to utilize every day! I was exposed to frameworks used to style and organize frontend pages such as Bootstrap and CSS-Grid. Additionally, we were given knowledge in various stacks such as MERN (React/Node.js), Django (Python), and .NET (C#).


Soon after graduating the bootcamp, I picked up my first software development contract building a dashboard website for a local jeep restoration company. After completing that, I continued to learn on my own, but started working as a Junior Cybersecurity Analyst within the ServiceNow toolset where I helped identify risks and establish the starting footprint of ISO 27001 into the organization. I missed building and creating and eventually switched back to a role as a backend applications developer using C#/.NET for a local grocery chain. After my time there, I found my way to my current position where I build and refactor ASP.NET legacy pages for an expansive set of web applications utilized by thousands of employees working across over 30 countries!
